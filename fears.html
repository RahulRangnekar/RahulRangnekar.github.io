This is my final project for UGBA 190T: Creativity Lab in the Spring 2017 semester. It is focused on a few of my deepest and darkest fears, and will be finished by the end of April. Check back for regular updates! Fear is such a large part of my everyday life, from relatively minor things like lifting too heavy of weight at the gym or asking a question in class, to the pervasive and lingering ones below. Throughout my junior year, I have made a conscious effort to acknowledge and assuage these fears. I examined them at the roots, and dug for the seeds. Through my personal creativity channels - graphic design, collage-making, and writing - I now impart to you why my fears exist and how I strive to make peace with them.


  • Professional Failure
  • Overcompensation
  • Neglecting Mental Health
  • Diminished Physical Health
  • Lifelong Solidarity
